About Me

Hi, My name is Ville.

I'm a Computer Vision and Machine Learning Engineer located in Espoo Finland with a MSc degree in Software and Telecommunication technology from University of Jyväskylä. I have obtained experience in developing and implementing customized detection solutions using computer vision techniques. My expertise includes image and video processing, building object detection and recognition solutions, while mainly using deep learning-based approaches. I previously worked as a Solution Consultant developing Machine Learning and DevOps solutions in different use cases for customers at Tietoevry. Currently I am pursuing a PhD degree in Computer Vision and working as a Doctoral Researcher at Aalto University in Content-Based Image and Information Retrieval Group, in the Department of Computer Science.

My interest in science lies in researching and developing new innovative AI solutions to obtain information from images and videos. In my thesis I studied the sensitivity of pretrained lightweight object detection models against the adversarial noise included in images. I chose this subject as it combined the security aspect and computer vision, which were two main study lines that I took courses during my studies. Prior to my thesis I worked as a Research Assistant in Faculty of Information Technology at University of Jyväskylä in Computer Vision Project which taught me the basics of research workflow. In future projects I would like to combine computer vision into sports and nature study to implement new solutions and obtain new information using this technology. My current research focuses on Remote Sensing data, Hyperspectral Imaging, Computer Vision and Deep Learning.

If you have any questions related to my projects or collaboration ideas etc. feel free to contact me through linkedin or email. I have selected some basic project solutions that I have built into this portfolio. It is still quite narrow but I am hoping to expanding it in the future with more meaningful projects :)

Lastly I would like to thank you for visiting at my site and I wish you have a good day!

Best regards,
Ville
